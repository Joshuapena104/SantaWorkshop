Santa's Workshop

By Joshua Pena

This is the night before Christmas. The night where every child is enjoying Christmas eve by the fire and watching Christmas movies with their families Santa and his elves are work hard to get these presents packed and ready to go so that every child could wake up to a present that Santa and his elves handmade and picked for them. All the presents are done being made and wrapped by the elves. Now, all that's left to do is load up Santa's magical red christmas bag. What makes it magical you might ask, but what makes Santa's bag magical is that it can fit any amount of gifts without getting full. When the elves are done packing this bag it would be ready to take off with Santa and his raindeers so that he is able to make every child have a merry and jolly Christmas.
